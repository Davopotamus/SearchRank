Submitting Feedback



Introduction



One of the best ways to get users coming to your site is to allow user feedback. Feedback helps designers understand the needs and wants of the audience you're trying to broadcast to and also lets the user know someone is behind the wheel of your content. There are many options available from free blog services to social media.

Free Blogs

Google offers free blog services on their site Blogger. This is only one of the many available free pre-designed systems setup to allow commenting and sharing. Engaging with your audience can help them feel like they are a part of the plan and helps them in having a helping hand in situations where you personally are looking for comments. When improving site rank, it helps to be active in the many means of communication as not everyone usesthe same sites. An audience is diverse when calling a site home.

Social Media

Google, Facebook and Twitter are among the many social media sites around today and make up over 90% of the total used social media platforms. These platforms make excellent commenting and sharing systems which are used worldwide. When running a business, your audience has the means to leave reviews, comment, share opinions and much more. Today, people online love to share their opinions to help others avoid or know who to choose when in need of service. The more platforms available, the more you have a chance to interact with your audience.
